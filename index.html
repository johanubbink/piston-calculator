Mounting points

Sash mount sits on the window; frame mount sits on the wall.
Both mounts lie on the surfaces, so at exactly 0° the strut lies flat in the wall plane and has zero leverage — the linkage is singular at fully-closed, so a latch (or hand) opens the first degree. The required force jumps to a finite value the instant it's cracked open, and stays finite through the rest of the travel.
